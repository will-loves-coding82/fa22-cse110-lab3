About Us

We are a team of 20 bright and talented individuals from across the globe.
As part of our mission to create amazing clothes that go above the industry
standards, we actively seek out the best materials, factories, supppers, and
distributors. Since 2015, we've sold over 2 milpon pieces and have been
featured in articles such as Vogue and received numerous titles including
Designer of the Year in the 2017 CFDA Awards
